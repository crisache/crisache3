Large experience observing at other telescopes, e.g. with the heterodyne receivers at the IRAM 30m telescope (Spain), with the bolometer array SHARC-II and heterodyne receivers at the CSO (Hawaii), observations with the 3mm heterodyne receiver at the Onsala 20m telescope (Sweden).
